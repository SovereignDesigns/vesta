FY23 Annual Results

Foresight Group Holding Limited announced our Annual Results for the Financial Year ending 31 March 2023.

FY23 Annual Report

    2023

    July: New sustainability data platform launched, powered by Salesforce and delivered by PwC

    2023

    June: Launch of first dedicated Sustainability Report

    2023

    June: Statkraft, Foresight and Progressive Energy form Grenian Hydrogen Limited, a new joint venture company

    2023

    February: Foresight Solar & Technology VCT plc sells its portfolio of assets held in the Ordinary share class

    2023

    January: Foresight Capital Management expands into the US with launch of sustainable infrastructure mutual fund with Cromwell Funds

    2022

    December: Foresight Sustainable Forestry Company becomes the first company to receive London Stock Exchange's Voluntary Carbon Market designation

    2022

    September: Foresight Group launches Dublin office and appoints team to oversee its Irish expansion

    2022

    July: Acquired Infrastructure Capital Holdings; adding approximately £3 billion to AUM and positioning Foresight as one of the leading independent renewable generation and infrastructure investors in Australia.

    2022

    June: Foresight appointed the Eden Project as its sustainability partner. Eden will advise Foresight on enhancing biodiversity across its portfolio with an initial focus on Infrastructure and Forestry assets.

    2022

    June: Acquired the technology ventures division of Downing LLP including the management of the Venture Capital Trust and Ventures Enterprise Investment Scheme businesses of Downing. This increased Foresight's private equity AUM to £1.2 billion.

    2022

    May-June: Launched three new funds to support UK and Irish SMEs: the Foresight West Yorkshire SME Investment Fund, the Foresight North East Fund and the AIB Foresight SME Impact Fund.

    2022

    March: FCM launched the FP Foresight Sustainable Future Themes Fund, providing investors with globally diversified exposure to sustainable development and decarbonisation sectors

    2021

    November: Shares in Foresight Sustainable Forestry Company Plc begin trading on the Main Market of the London Stock Exchange

    2021

    September: Foresight Energy Infrastructure Partners (“FEIP”) exceeds target by 70% with final close at €851.4 million and a total capital pool in excess of €1.0 billion.

    2021

    February: Shares in Foresight Group Holdings Limited begin trading on the Main Market of the London Stock Exchange.

    2020

    December: Strengthened European presence with new Alternative Investment Fund Manager (“AIFM”) office located in Luxembourg.

    2020

    September: Completed first investment into full fibre network in East Sussex with the potential to deliver hyperfast broadband to over 100,000 homes and businesses.

    2020

    August: Completed acquisition of £1.8bn (at completion) PiP Manager Limited (“PiP”).

    2020

    April: Completed Foresight Energy Infrastructure Partners (“FEIP”) investment into 231MW Greenfield wind farm project (Skaftäsen) in Sweden.

    2020

    January: Launched Foresight Energy Infrastructure Partners (“FEIP”), our first European-targeted, sustainability-led energy infrastructure fund, securing commitments of €342 million.

    2019

    Completed acquisition of the advisory mandate and management team of £764m JLEN Environmental Assets Group ("JLEN"). Launched the £100 million East of England Fund and the £20 million Scottish Growth Fund with offices in Cambridge and Edinburgh.

    2019

    Foresight and Williams Advanced Engineering launched the Foresight Williams Technology VCT share class, which sits within the Foresight Solar & Technology VCT plc.

    2018

    Appointed by the British Business Bank as equity fund manager for Midlands Engine Investment Fund, opened UK offices in Leicester and Milton Keynes and international office in Spain (Madrid) to focus on solar investments.

    2017

    Established Foresight Capital Management which manages three OEICs: FP Foresight UK Infrastructure Income Fund ("FIIF"), FP Foresight Global Real Infrastructure Fund ("GRIF") and FP Foresight Sustainable Real Estate Fund ("REF").

    2016

    Foresight and Williams Advanced Engineering started their unique investment collaboration with the launch of the Foresight Williams Technology EIS Fund.

    2016

    Opened office in Australia (Sydney) to focus on solar investments.

    2015

    Launched £58 million North West focused Foresight Regional Investment LP and opened Manchester Office.

    2014

    Relocated UK principal office to London in the Shard.

    2013

    Listed Foresight Solar Fund Ltd. ("FSFL") on the London Stock Exchange, and launched a £40 million regional Nottingham based private equity fund backed by the British Business Bank.

    2012

    Awarded a primary mandate of £50 million by the UK Green Investment Bank.

    2011

    Launched Foresight Inheritance Tax Solution ("ITS") and the £60.6 million Foresight Environmental Fund ("FEF") - with cornerstone investment by the European Investment Bank.

    2008

    Opened first international office (Rome) to focus on Southern European solar investments.

    2007

    Made first environmental investment and established solar infrastructure team.

    1997

    Launched Foresight VCT (formerly Foresight Technology VCT) - the first retail product, and second overall product, launched by the Foresight Group.

    1984

    Founded by Bernard Fairman and Peter English to manage a £20 million fund sponsored by Robert Fleming.

    Our Managers

      Foresight Group LLP

      Foresight Group LLP has been authorised as a Full-Scope Alternative Investment Fund Manager ("AIFM") with the Financial Conduct Authority (“FCA”) since 2001 and the US Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”) since 2021 and acts as AIFM and Investment Adviser to various funds across the Infrastructure and Private Equity divisions of the business. It also acts as Investment Manager to our open ended, UCITS investment management division, Foresight Capital Management. It is the principal asset manager within the Foresight Group. Foresight Group LLP’s authorisations and admissions to act as an AIFM are in accordance with Part 4A of the Financial Services and Markets Act (FSMA) 2000. The full list of Foresight Group LLP’s permitted activities can be found on the Financial Services Register . Its authorisations to act as a registered investment adviser is in accordance with the US Investment Advisers Act of 1940. The Investment Adviser Firm Summary on Foresight Group LLP’s SEC registration can be found via the Broker Check website at www.brokercheck.finra.org
